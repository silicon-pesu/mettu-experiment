Since 2021, we have been pushing boundaries of hardware-software co-design. Our club is united by shared interest for embedded, VLSI and analog.

**But what do we actually build?**

We are dedicated to the fundamentals of electronics and _embedded systems_. Our work spans from designing analog circuits like OTA and building line-following robotics to exploring embedded project development. We are also actively exploring the VLSI domain.

To see what we are currently working on, check out our [Projects](/projects/) - our open repository of build logs and hardware documentation! 💚
